Multi School Coverage

PPSBathrooms has real time coverage for three high schools in the Portland Public Schools area, Cleveland High School, Franklin High School and Ida B Wells High School.

Simple Fast Data for Students

PPSBathrooms provides real time data on the availability of bathrooms at high schools across Portland Public schools. Each bathroom has a corresponding square next to it, green representing an open bathroom, and red representing a bathroom that has been closed.

A Tool to Keep Students in Class

With bathrooms consistently closed from vandalism or smoking, students spend excessive time outside of class finding open bathrooms. PPSBathrooms fixes this issue, providing students with a resource that eliminates the need to go from floor to floor searching for a bathroom.
